More information
I tweaked and retrained Open AI's GPT-2 on the lyrics of some of my favorite rap artists. I then generated lyrics with the trained models and chose some of the best output to share. I aggregated the lyrics for each artist using Selenium and Genius.com.

My first idea was to use RNNs and LSTMs and create and train the model from scratch. This was simply not possible as the corpus for each artist was only tens of thousands of lines. In comparison, GPT-2 was trained on millions of text samples. Simply put, no model I created on my own would be at the same caliber as a pretrained language model. Thus, I was forced to trash all the work I had done with RNNs and LSTMs.

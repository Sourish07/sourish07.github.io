About me

Bio

Hey! I'm an 18 year-old who's striving to build their career around their passion for computer science. I love working on projects which allow me to apply the knowlege I've acquired in the classroom while simultaneously obtaining skills which I didn't expected to learn. Outside of computer science, I have a vested interest in public speaking, international politics, and economics. In my free time, if I'm not with friends, I'm either playing video games or reading about and researching topics I'm passionate about to continue expanding my knowledge base.

Education

I'm currently studying at the University of Wisconsin-Madison pursuing a double major in computer science and economics. I'm invloved in several clubs on campus such the Federal Reserve Challenge, LEAD @Econ, and the Wisconsin International Review.
